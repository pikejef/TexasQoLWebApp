Clarification

Unrecorded and suppressed data values in our sources: Some of the data sources for quality of life variables included a minimal degree of unrecorded and/or suppressed data values for some counties.

These include:

  1. Crimes
    • 8 unrecorded values for: Cottle, Culberson, Dickens, Dimmit, Kent, Kinney, McMullen, and Stonewall County
  2. Deaths
    • 3 suppressed values for: Borden, Kenedy, and King County

A generalized data value input was provided for these counties in their respective variable columns. Research was conducted for the Crimes variable, and documentation provided for suppressed values by the source was referred to for the Deaths variable.


Additional Disclaimers

  1. Hospitals: Includes only “Acute and Psychiatric Care” Hospitals
  2. Weather and Climate Risk Score: Index score was rounded to two decimal places
  3. State Parks: The Texas Parks & Wildlife Department had their own semantics for what was deemed as a “park”
    • For example: According to the Texas Parks & Wildlife Department's datasheet, Denton County apparently has 9 parks. In reality, these 9 "parks" are actually 9 different units of a larger, single park, being Ray Roberts Lake State Park. For this web application, each park was counted only once–not by units, trailways, etc.–when giving each county a state park count value.
